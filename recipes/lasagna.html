Lasagna Recipe

Description

A delish lasagna with meat and cheese

Follow the recipe for this easy dish!

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef mince
  • lasagna noods
  • 250g mozz cheese
  • 250g rigoat
  • 250g tomato puree
  • 1 chopped medium onion
  • garlic
  • basil
  • oregano
  • salt & pepper

Steps to make

  1. Salt and boil pasta
  2. brown beef mince with garlic and chopped onions
  3. drain beef, add tomato puree
  4. mix rigoat and beef mixture in bowl
  5. place noods in a bowl and layer meat rigoat mixture on top
  6. repeat until done
  7. top with cheese
  8. place in oven on 450F for 20mins
  9. remove from oven and cool for 10mins before cutting
  10. Enjoy! Buon Apetito!
